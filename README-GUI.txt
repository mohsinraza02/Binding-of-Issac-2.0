How to play: You are a university student and studying for a final exam. Your goal is to pick up as much resources within a limited amount of time. 
Resources will prepare you for the exam so make sure you get as much as you can before the exam starts! In the final battle, you and the test will take turns on attacking. 
Your attack stats increases your attack damage and defense decreases the damage you take. Kill the test before it kills you to pass! Good luck and have fun.

HOW TO RUN AND COMPILE:
1) Code for GUI is located in "GUI-CODE" BRANCH.
2) Compile the code and run Main.java
